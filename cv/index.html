DAVID MERINO RECALDE

Phd Researcher, Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona

ABOUT ME

I am a PhD researcher specializing in Spanish Golden Age theatre, with a focus on the transmission, reception, and cultural impact of dramatic works. My doctoral project combines digital scholarly editing, archival research, and data modeling to explore how texts such as Fuenteovejuna have been preserved, interpreted, and staged across history.

With an interdisciplinary background in theatre practice, philology, and digital humanities, I apply computational methods to study drama as both a literary and performative phenomenon. My expertise includes digital editing (XML-TEI), web development, network analysis, and the design of ontological models and data infrastructures for cultural heritage

.

SOFTWARE & TOOLS DEVELOPED

2025

feniX-ML

Developed with Anna Abate and Emanuele Leboffe
Biblioteca Digital PROLOPE project
https://github.com/prolopeuab/feniX-ML
Desktop application that transforms critical editions of Spanish Golden Age plays from DOCX into valid XML-TEI, supporting multiple input files (text, notes, critical apparatus, metadata). It generates structured TEI files with a complete teiHeader and provides both raw XML and interactive HTML previews using CETEIcean.js.
